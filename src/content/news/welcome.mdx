We are pleased to launch the new Nicolas James Environmental website, designed to give procurement teams, contract managers, and asset owners a clear, comprehensive view of our capability.

## What You'll Find

Our new site provides detailed information on each of our core services, from vegetation management and arboriculture through to emergency response and NRSWA traffic management. You can explore our capability by service type or by the sector you operate in.

## Built for Procurement

We have designed the site with procurement audiences in mind. You will find our accreditations, staff qualifications, and compliance documentation readily accessible, along with case studies that demonstrate our delivery approach with measurable outcomes.
