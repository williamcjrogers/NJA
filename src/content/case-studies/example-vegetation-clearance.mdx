## The Challenge

The client required a comprehensive vegetation clearance programme across multiple operational sites, including treatment works, pumping stations, and access corridors. Vegetation had been allowed to encroach on operational areas, restricting access for maintenance teams and creating biosecurity risks. Works needed to be completed within a defined programme window without disrupting ongoing site operations.

## Our Solution

We deployed directly employed teams with our own specialist equipment, including the Baroness bank mower for steep embankments and commercial brush cutters for dense undergrowth. PA1/PA6 qualified operatives applied targeted herbicide treatment to prevent regrowth in critical operational areas.

All works were delivered under a structured programme with daily toolbox talks, site-specific risk assessments, and regular progress reporting to the client. Where watercourse vegetation was included, our Water Safety Level 2 qualified teams carried appropriate safety equipment.

## The Outcome

The programme was completed on schedule across all 12 sites with zero safety incidents. Operational access was fully restored, and a planned maintenance schedule was agreed for ongoing vegetation management, converting reactive spend into a predictable, planned programme.
