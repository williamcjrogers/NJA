## The Challenge

Severe weather caused multiple fallen trees across a local authority area, blocking roads, damaging fencing, and obstructing access to residential areas. The client needed rapid response across multiple locations simultaneously, with NRSWA-compliant traffic management for highway works.

## Our Solution

We mobilised within two hours of the initial call, deploying NPTC-qualified chainsaw teams with our Chapter 8-equipped vehicles for immediate highway clearance. Traffic management was established at each location before tree work commenced, ensuring public safety throughout.

Our teams worked across eight separate locations over a 48-hour period, systematically clearing fallen trees, removing debris, and making sites safe. Damaged boundary fencing was temporarily secured and reported for follow-up repair.

## The Outcome

All eight locations were cleared within 48 hours, restoring road access and removing immediate hazards. The speed and quality of response led to an ongoing framework agreement for emergency and reactive works with the authority.
