## Overview

We deliver large-scale vegetation management and clearance across infrastructure corridors, operational sites, watercourses, and public estate. Our teams combine mechanised and manual methods to manage vegetation safely and efficiently in live, regulated environments.

From planned seasonal programmes to reactive clearance requirements, we provide a reliable, self-delivered service using our own equipment and directly employed operatives.

## How We Deliver

Our vegetation management capability covers the full scope of works required by infrastructure asset owners:

- **Mechanised clearance** using specialist equipment including our Baroness bank mower for steep gradients and embankments
- **Manual clearance** by NPTC-qualified operatives for selective vegetation removal in sensitive areas
- **Herbicide application** by PA1/PA6 certified operatives for programmed weed and vegetation control
- **Seasonal maintenance programmes** tailored to asset owner requirements and environmental constraints
- **Emergency and reactive clearance** for storm damage, fallen vegetation, and access obstruction

All works are delivered with full NRSWA traffic management capability where roadside or highway access is required.

## Why Choose NJE

- **Self-delivered capability** — our own teams and specialist equipment, not sub-contracted
- **Qualified operatives** — NPTC, PA1/PA6, CSCS, and SMSTS qualified across the team
- **Specialist equipment** — Baroness bank mower, commercial brush cutters, and full vegetation management kit owned in-house
- **Scalable delivery** — core capability for day-to-day operations, with rapid scaling through hired plant for larger programmes
- **Live environment specialists** — trained and equipped for working safely in operational, public, and highway environments
