## Overview

We deliver specialist vegetation management and clearance for watercourses, drainage channels, culverts, and flood defence assets. Our teams hold Working Safely in Water Level 2 qualifications and carry appropriate water safety equipment on every operation, ensuring safe, compliant delivery in and around water environments.

## How We Deliver

- **Watercourse vegetation clearance** — manual and mechanised removal of growth obstructing flow and access
- **Drainage channel and culvert clearance** — maintaining drainage capacity and preventing flood risk
- **Bankside vegetation management** — planned programmes to manage growth and maintain structural integrity
- **Herbicide application** — PA1/PA6 certified aquatic and near-water herbicide treatment
- **Emergency response** — rapid deployment for blocked watercourses and flood-related incidents

## Why Choose NJE

- **Water Safety Level 2** — teams trained and equipped for safe working in and around watercourses
- **PA1/PA6 certified** — qualified for herbicide application in aquatic and near-water environments
- **Self-contained teams** — life jackets, throw lines, and water rescue equipment carried on every operation
- **Environmental awareness** — understanding of ecological constraints and seasonal working restrictions
- **Emergency capability** — 24/7 availability for watercourse blockage and flood response
