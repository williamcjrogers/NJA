## Overview

Our arboriculture service covers the full range of tree works required by infrastructure asset owners, local authorities, and housing providers. All tree work is carried out by NPTC-qualified operatives with the equipment, competence, and safety management to work in live, regulated environments.

## How We Deliver

- **Tree felling and sectional dismantling** in confined and sensitive locations
- **Crown reduction, thinning, and lifting** to maintain clearances and manage growth
- **Dead-wooding and hazard tree management** to protect public safety and infrastructure
- **Stump grinding and treatment** for complete removal where required
- **Emergency tree work** — rapid response to storm-damaged and fallen trees

All arboriculture operations are supported by our in-house NRSWA traffic management capability for works on or adjacent to the highway network.

## Why Choose NJE

- **NPTC-qualified teams** — certified in chainsaw operations, tree felling, and aerial tree work
- **Emergency response** — 24/7 availability for storm damage and fallen tree incidents
- **Traffic management** — integrated Chapter 8-compliant highway working capability
- **Self-delivered** — our own operatives and equipment, maintaining quality control on every site
- **Infrastructure experience** — experienced in tree works within utility corridors, highway networks, and operational environments
