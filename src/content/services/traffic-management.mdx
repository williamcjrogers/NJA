## Overview

We deliver NRSWA-compliant traffic management for all roadside and highway works. Our Chapter 8-equipped vehicles, qualified operatives, and in-house signage capability enable us to create safe working zones for vegetation management, tree works, fencing, and general site works on or adjacent to the highway network.

## How We Deliver

- **Chapter 8 traffic management** — fully compliant vehicle and pedestrian traffic management
- **NRSWA-qualified operatives** — trained in the setup and management of roadside working zones
- **Chapter 8-equipped vehicles** — flatbed and 4x4 vehicles with high-visibility chevrons, beacon bars, and highway signage
- **Permit and notice management** — handling of street works notices and permit applications
- **Integrated delivery** — traffic management provided as part of vegetation, tree, and site works packages

## Why Choose NJE

- **NRSWA qualified** — operatives and supervisors trained under the New Roads and Street Works Act
- **Chapter 8 equipped** — our own compliant vehicles and signage, not hired in
- **Integrated service** — traffic management as standard with all roadside and highway operations
- **Compliant delivery** — proper notice management and permit coordination
- **Self-contained** — no reliance on third-party traffic management providers for day-to-day operations
