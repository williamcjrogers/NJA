## Overview

We deliver soft dig excavation, backfill, and surface reinstatement for utility and infrastructure works. Our teams work carefully around buried services, cables, and pipelines in live operational environments, providing clean, compliant reinstatement to the required standards.

## How We Deliver

- **Soft dig excavation** — hand and vacuum excavation methods for safe working around buried services
- **Trench backfill** — compacted backfill to specification using appropriate materials
- **Surface reinstatement** — restoration of hard and soft surfaces to pre-work condition or specification
- **Topsoil and seeding** — reinstatement of grassed and vegetated areas following works
- **Site clearance** — removal of spoil, materials, and debris to leave a clean, safe site

## Why Choose NJE

- **CSCS and SMSTS qualified** — competent teams for working in live infrastructure environments
- **Plant capability** — digger-trained operatives with plant tickets for mechanised earthworks
- **Careful working** — experienced in operating around buried services and sensitive assets
- **Integrated delivery** — soft dig provided alongside vegetation clearance, fencing, and reinstatement as a complete package
- **Quality reinstatement** — surfaces restored to standard, minimising follow-up works
