## Overview

We deliver estate-wide grounds maintenance programmes for infrastructure operators, local authorities, and housing providers. Our service covers planned, repeatable maintenance to defined standards, using commercial-grade equipment operated by qualified, directly employed teams.

## How We Deliver

- **Planned mowing programmes** using commercial mowers and our specialist Baroness bank mower for steep and difficult terrain
- **Hedge cutting and boundary maintenance** on programmed schedules
- **Strimming, edging, and hard surface clearance** to maintain site presentation
- **Herbicide application** by PA1/PA6 certified operatives for weed control and vegetation management
- **Litter picking and site cleansing** as part of integrated maintenance programmes
- **Seasonal works** including leaf clearance, winter preparation, and spring reinstatement

## Why Choose NJE

- **Specialist equipment** — including Baroness bank mower for terrain other contractors cannot access
- **Planned programmes** — scheduled, repeatable maintenance delivered to measurable standards
- **Qualified teams** — PA1/PA6 herbicide application, CSCS, and relevant grounds maintenance certifications
- **Self-delivered** — our own equipment and operatives for reliable, consistent service delivery
- **Scalable** — core capability for day-to-day programmes, with rapid scaling for larger estate requirements
