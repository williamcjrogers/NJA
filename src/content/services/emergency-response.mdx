## Overview

We provide 24/7 emergency and reactive response for storm damage, fallen trees, blocked watercourses, damaged fencing, and access clearance. Our self-contained teams mobilise rapidly with owned equipment to make sites safe, restore access, and protect infrastructure assets.

## How We Deliver

- **Fallen tree removal** — emergency clearance of fallen and dangerous trees from roads, properties, and infrastructure
- **Blocked watercourse clearance** — urgent removal of debris and vegetation causing flood risk
- **Damaged fencing repair** — rapid reinstatement of security and boundary fencing
- **Access clearance** — removing obstructions to restore safe access to sites and infrastructure
- **Storm damage management** — coordinated response to widespread weather-related incidents

## Why Choose NJE

- **24/7 availability** — emergency response around the clock, every day of the year
- **Rapid mobilisation** — self-contained teams with owned equipment ready to deploy at short notice
- **Qualified teams** — NPTC chainsaw, Water Safety Level 2, and NRSWA qualified for any emergency scenario
- **Traffic management** — integrated Chapter 8 highway capability for roadside emergency works
- **Single point of contact** — one call for trees, watercourses, fencing, and access clearance
