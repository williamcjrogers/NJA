## Overview

We install, replace, and repair security, boundary, and stock fencing across operational infrastructure sites, housing estates, and public land. Our fencing capability covers planned installation programmes and reactive repair for storm and incident-damaged fencing.

## How We Deliver

- **Security fencing** — installation of palisade, mesh, and anti-climb fencing for operational sites
- **Boundary fencing** — close board, panel, and post-and-rail fencing for estates and public areas
- **Stock fencing** — post-and-wire and stock-proof fencing for agricultural and rural infrastructure
- **Gate installation** — pedestrian and vehicle access gates with appropriate ironmongery
- **Reactive repair** — rapid response to storm-damaged, vandal-damaged, and deteriorated fencing

## Why Choose NJE

- **Self-delivered** — our own teams with dedicated fencing tools and equipment
- **Planned and reactive** — both programmed installation and emergency repair capability
- **Infrastructure experience** — experienced in fencing for utilities, housing, and public estate
- **Integrated service** — fencing delivered alongside vegetation management, grounds maintenance, and site works
- **CSCS qualified** — teams holding appropriate site safety certifications
